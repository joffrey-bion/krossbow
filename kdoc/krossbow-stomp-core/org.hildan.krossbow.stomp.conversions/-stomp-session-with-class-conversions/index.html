Content copied to clipboard

Returns a cold Flow of Ts that subscribes on collect, and unsubscribes when the consuming coroutine is cancelled. The received MESSAGE frames are converted to instances of T or null, but the exact conversion is implementation-dependent. Message frames without a body MAY be skipped or result in an exception depending on the implementation, they don't have to be translated into null values. Conversely, message frames with a non-empty body may result in null values in the flow depending on the implementation.
